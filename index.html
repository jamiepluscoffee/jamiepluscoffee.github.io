Songkick

I began working in startups in 2011 when I interned for Songkick's community support team.
I loved it so much that I convinced them to give me a full time role and went about learning everything I could about product, growth, sales and getting shit done.
5 Years later I left Songkick to start my own startup.

Awne

For 9 months I founded and worked on a start-up in the relationship health space. The problem we set out to solve was - in a world where there are 1000’s of dating apps why is there nothing to help keep relationships healthy once you’ve found one? Through working on this problem we;

  • Ran countless user interviews with couples, people who had gone through couple therapy and couple therapists themselves
  • Prototyped and tested wireframes, mockups and a concierge MVPs as we iterated towards a solution.
  • Pitched many of the top Angels in UK & EU ecosystem.

    • It was a great experience and I learned a lot but unfortunately we didn't see the results we needed to take it to continue (#failfast).
