My Work

Songkick

I began working in startups as an intern for Songkick's community support team. I loved the team and worked my ass off and was rewarded with the offer of a full time role. Over the next four and a half years I worked on or was responsible for a number of projects including:

  • Launching Songkick Detour, a crowdfunding platform for concerts.
  • Introducing Songkick's primary ticketing and scaling it to over 30% of concerts in the UK.
  • Product partnership deals with the likes of Spotify, Shazam and Pandora.
  • Running a hack-day for the whole 140 person company, (including all non-technical staff).

    • Between all of that (and spending a fair amount of time on planes) I learned everything I could about product, growth, sales and getting shit done. I then made the hard decision to leave Songkick and try building my own startup.
