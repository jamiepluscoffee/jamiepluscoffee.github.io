Hi, I'm Jamie.

A passionate, curious generalist who has worked in startups for the past 6 years.

Songkick

I began working in startups in 2011 when I interned for Songkick's community support team.
I loved it so much that I convinced them to give me a full time role and went about learning everything I could about product, growth, sales and getting shit done.
5 Years later I left Songkick to start my own startup.

Awne

For 9 months I founded and worked on a start-up in the relationship health space. The problem we set out to solve was - in world where there are 1000’s of dating apps why is there nothing to help keep relationships healthy when you’ve found one? Through working on this problem we;

  • Ran 30+ user interviews with couples, people who had gone through couple therapy and couple therapists themselves
  • Prototyped and tested wireframes, mockups and a concierge MVPs as we iterated towards a solution.
  • Pitched many of the top Angels in UK & EU ecosystem.
    • It was a great experience and I learned a lot but unfortunately we didn't see the results we needed to take it to continue (#failfast).

    Now

    After earning my first startup scars I'm currently freelancing as a product lead, helping companies make sure the product they are building is problem and user focused and occasionally digging them out of the kind of holes you get in to when you focus on shipping a product not a solution.
